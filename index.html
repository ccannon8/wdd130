Idaho

Idaho is a mountainous state in the Pacific Northwest, known for its stunning natural landscapes, including vast forests, rivers, and rugged mountain ranges. It is famous for its potato production and offers abundant outdoor recreational activities like hiking, skiing, and whitewater rafting. Idaho's economy is a mix of agriculture, manufacturing, and technology, with Boise serving as the state’s capital and largest city.
